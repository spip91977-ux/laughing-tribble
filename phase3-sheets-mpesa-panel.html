🟢 Step 1 — Create a Google Cloud Service Account
A Service Account lets your server write to Google Sheets automatically without any browser login.
1
Go to Google Cloud Console
Visit console.cloud.google.com → Create a new project named KJ-LandSurveyors
2
Enable Google Sheets API
APIs & Services → Library → search "Google Sheets API" → Enable it
3
Create Service Account
APIs & Services → Credentials → Create Credentials → Service Account → name it kj-sheets-agent
4
Download JSON key
Click your service account → Keys → Add Key → JSON → Download. Open the file and copy the client_email and private_key values.
5
Create the Spreadsheet
Go to sheets.google.com → New spreadsheet → name it KJ Land Surveyors Operations → Copy the ID from the URL (between /d/ and /edit)
6
Share with Service Account
In the spreadsheet → Share → paste your client_email from the JSON → Editor access → Done
📋 Enter Your Google Credentials
Paste your values from the downloaded JSON key file.
Add to your .env file:
GOOGLE_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_EMAIL=paste-here GOOGLE_PRIVATE_KEY="-----BEGIN RSA PRIVATE KEY-----\nYOUR_KEY\n-----END RSA PRIVATE KEY-----" GOOGLE_SPREADSHEET_ID=paste-spreadsheet-id-here
🟢 Step 1 — Apply for M-Pesa Daraja API
Daraja is Safaricom's official developer API. The sandbox is free and instant — production requires business registration.
1
Register on Daraja Portal
Go to developer.safaricom.co.ke → Register with your business email → Verify your account
2
Create an App (Sandbox — instant)
My Apps → Add New App → Select APIs: M-Pesa Express (STK Push), Customer To Business → Create. You get Consumer Key + Secret immediately.
3
Get your Passkey
In sandbox, use the test passkey provided on the portal. For production, Safaricom emails you a unique passkey.
4
Apply for Production (Business)
Go Live → fill in your business details, KRA PIN, Paybill/Till number. Safaricom reviews in 2–5 business days. You need: Certificate of Incorporation or Business Permit.
5
Get your Shortcode
Your Paybill or Till number from Safaricom Business. For sandbox testing, use 174379 as the shortcode.
📋 Enter Your Daraja Credentials
From your Daraja developer portal app page.
Add to your .env file:
MPESA_CONSUMER_KEY=paste-here MPESA_CONSUMER_SECRET=paste-here MPESA_SHORTCODE=174379 MPESA_PASSKEY=paste-here MPESA_ENVIRONMENT=sandbox MPESA_CALLBACK_URL=https://your-server.com/mpesa/callback
📊 Daily AI Summary — How It Works
Every morning at 7:00 AM East Africa Time, the system automatically:
①
Counts all activity from the past 24 hours
New clients, project stage changes, payments received, revenue collected
②
Claude AI writes a briefing
The Reporting Agent reads all the data and writes a professional 4–6 sentence daily briefing, flagging anything urgent
③
Synced to "Daily Summary" Google Sheet tab
One row per day: date, stats, full AI narrative — your permanent operations record
④
Sent to Director via WhatsApp
You wake up to a full briefing on your phone. No need to log in anywhere.
Example WhatsApp Message You'll Receive:
